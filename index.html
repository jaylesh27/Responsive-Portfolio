About Me


Welcome to my (nascent) website! My name is Jaylesh Patel, but call me Jay for short. I am an aspiring full-stack web developer and am currently enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. If you're interested in my "origin story", then here it is: I graduated from Rutgers Business School in 2013 with degrees in finance and supply chain management. After a few years in the supply chain field, I decided to change careers and pursue a long-held interest of mine: coding!

Apart from coding, I do have other interests. I enjoy hiking, running, reading, keeping up to date with events in both politics and tech, and travelling. I've been to quite a few countries already (the U.K., France, Germany, Italy, Switzerland, Malaysia, India, Singapore...to name a few).
